Who is Rasel

THE VOICE
BEHIND THE TAKE.

Rasel is an independent content creator and political analyst producing sharp, context-driven commentary on the issues that define our era — from parliamentary politics and electoral battles to the quiet power shifts reshaping the global order.

"Every headline has a history. Every data point tells a story. My job is to make that story impossible to ignore."

Across Instagram, X/Twitter, and YouTube, Rasel brings research-backed insight to audiences who want more than talking points. Whether it's the tectonic shifts in South Asian geopolitics or the way AI is rewiring economies, the analysis is always grounded, never sensational.
